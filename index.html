Color Distribution Analysis

Examples of regex filters:

  • green: All colors that have 'green' in their name.
  • \bdark\b: All colors that have 'dark' as a word in their name (e.g., 'dark green', 'dark blue').
  • ^[a-zA-Z]+$: All colors whose name is a single word (only alphabetic characters).
  • yellow|orange: All colors that have 'yellow' or 'orange' in their name.
  • ^p.*e$: All colors that start with 'p' and end with 'e' in their name.
